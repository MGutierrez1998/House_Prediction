What We Do

The product is an application in which the customers will be able to view the property prices with various options such as area, type of property and number of beds, baths,etc. for different price ranges.

Target Population

Our product is targeted at both buyers and sellers:

Client House Buyer: Given the recent social distancing law, the client will not be able to inspect and compare different prices of properties in the same area. However, the client can use our product to browse different locations and prices. With this new finding, they can understand which features (such as number of beds, car parks,etc.) affect different property prices.

Client House Seller: This is considered to be for the average house-flipper. This client will be able to take advantage of a feature that affects a house price the most. Such a house will be bought only to be sold quickly at a much higher price due to the market saturation. This method will allow a client to invest in a small cost to gain a large return.
